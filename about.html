About Bunk Calculator

Bunk Calculator is a free tool built for college students to help them track their attendance and figure out exactly how many classes they can skip — without falling below the 75% attendance requirement.

Why We Built This

Every college student has been there — wondering "can I bunk today's class or not?" Manually calculating attendance is confusing and time-consuming. Bunk Calculator solves this in seconds.

How It Works

Simply enter your current attendance percentage and total classes conducted. The calculator instantly tells you:

  • How many classes you have missed
  • How many more classes you can safely skip
  • How many classes you need to attend to recover attendance
  • What happens to your attendance if you bunk the next class

Who Is This For

This tool is built for engineering and college students in India where 75% attendance is mandatory in most universities.

Free & No Login Required

Bunk Calculator is completely free to use. No sign up, no login, no personal data collected. Just open and calculate.
